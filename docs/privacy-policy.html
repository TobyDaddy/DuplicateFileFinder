Privacy Policy – Duplicate File Finder Tool

Effective date: 2025-08-12

This privacy policy explains how Duplicate File Finder Tool handles your data. We take your privacy seriously.

What data we collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal data (PII). The app does not include telemetry, analytics, advertising, or third‑party SDKs.

How the app works

  • All operations run locally on your device.
  • The app only accesses file paths you select to scan for duplicate files.
  • The app does not upload your files or metadata to any server.
  • No background network communication is performed.

Permissions

The app may request access to the local file system you choose. This is solely to scan and manage duplicate files as requested by you.

Children's privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

Contact

If you have any questions, contact us at lirincy@gmail.com.
